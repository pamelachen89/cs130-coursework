Design Inspiration

  • JJK Jukebox Fic Fest: I like the fonts and colors here!
  • Haikyuu Map: I really enjoy the futuristic feel of this and how it feels like everything is suspended in space because of the vibrant colors against the empty black
  • Apple: I enjoy the minimalism and symmetry that Apple uses on their websites
  • Fanart: Although this veers more on the side of art than design, I really admire how these pieces utilize color theory to pick shades that either contrast or complement each other
  • Tumblr Log-in Page: Perhaps a basic choice, but I really like how the Tumblr landing page always chooses a random piece of art as its background. I also like the animations they are using.
  • Hippo Campus' Bambi: I like how the title is half black, half pink and the fonts that they used to give it a retro feel
